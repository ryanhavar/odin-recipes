Hamburger

Description

This vegan Beyond Meat burger is incredible! The patty is made from Beyond Meat and it tastes so juicy and flavorful. We season it just right and cook it until it's crispy on the outside but still tender on the inside. To finish it off, we add all the classic toppings like lettuce, tomato, onion, and a sauce of your choice. And all of that is nestled between two fluffy buns. It's the perfect bite! You have to try it.

Ingredients

  • Beyond Meat patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Sauce (e.g. ketchup, mustard, mayo, hot sauce, etc.)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the patty: Heat a pan over medium heat and cook the Beyond Meat patty until crispy on the outside and no longer pink in the middle, about 3-5 minutes per side.
  2. Toast the buns: Toast the buns in the oven or on a pan until they're lightly golden and crispy.
  3. Assemble the burger: Place the cooked Beyond Meat patty on one of the buns, followed by lettuce, tomato, onion, and your choice of sauce. Sprinkle with salt and pepper, if desired.
  4. Serve and enjoy: Top with the remaining bun and serve immediately, while everything is still hot and fresh.
