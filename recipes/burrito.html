Burrito

Description

This vegan burrito is a delicious and satisfying wrap made with seasoned black beans, sautéed vegetables such as bell peppers and onions, and seasoned tofu or tempeh, all rolled up in a warm flour tortilla. It's topped off with a layer of guacamole or salsa and sprinkled with vegan cheese, before rolled up tightly. The result is a hearty and flavorful dish, perfect for those following a plant-based diet or anyone who loves tasty and nutritious food. Optional additions like rice, corn, or hot sauce can also be added to customize the flavor to your liking.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortilla
  • Black beans
  • Vegetables such as bell peppers and onions
  • Seasoning for the filling ingredients
  • Tofu or tempeh
  • Guacamole or salsa
  • Vegan cheese
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Avocado
  • Rice
  • Corn
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Sauté the vegetables: Cut up your choice of vegetables, such as bell peppers and onions, and cook them in a pan until they are tender.
  2. Season the filling: Season the sautéed vegetables with your desired spices, and add in cooked black beans and either seasoned tofu or tempeh. Cook everything until heated through and well combined.
  3. Warm the tortilla: Place a large flour tortilla on a griddle or in a dry pan, and warm it until it's pliable.
  4. Add the sauce: Spread a layer of guacamole or salsa on the center of the tortilla.
  5. Assemble the burrito: Spoon the filling ingredients on top of the sauce, then sprinkle with shredded vegan cheese.
  6. Roll the burrito: Roll the tortilla tightly, tucking in the sides as you go, to create a burrito.
  7. Serve: Serve the burrito hot, garnished with additional salsa, fresh cilantro, or diced avocado, if desired.
