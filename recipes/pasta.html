Pasta

Description

This Lemony Basil Creamy Vegan Pasta with Broccoli & Sun-dried Tomatoes is a delicious and flavorful dish that is sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters.

The sauce is combined with steamed broccoli florets and sun-dried tomatoes for a burst of flavor and texture in every bite. The dish is finished with a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a touch of heat, making for a perfect balance of flavors that is both fresh and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Cashews
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh basil
  • Broccoli florets
  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil spaghetti in a pot of salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Make the sauce: Blend cashews, lemon juice, and fresh basil in a blender or food processor until smooth and creamy.
  3. Steam the broccoli: Steam broccoli florets until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Assemble the dish: In a large serving bowl, mix together the cooked pasta, creamy sauce, steamed broccoli, and sun-dried tomatoes.
  5. Finish and serve: Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and a pinch of salt, and toss everything together. Serve hot and enjoy!
